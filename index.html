Our Story

Over 50 Years of
Home-Style Cooking

Tucked right on Mill Street in the heart of Almonte, Superior Restaurant has been a cornerstone of this community for over half a century. We're not fancy — and that's exactly the point.

Pull up a stool at the counter or slide into a booth. You'll find paper placemats, friendly faces, and plates piled high with honest food at honest prices. From all-day breakfast to legendary fried chicken, homemade pizza, Italian specialties, and fresh seafood — every dish comes with the warmth of a place that genuinely cares.

Whether you're a local popping in for Tuesday eggs, or a cottage-goer swinging through Lanark Highlands — welcome back. You've been missed.
